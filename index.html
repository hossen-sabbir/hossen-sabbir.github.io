About Me

ðŸ‘‹ Hi! I am Sabbir Hossen

I am a recent Computer Science graduate from Bangladesh University with a strong foundation in Machine Learning, and I am currently working as a Research Assistant at the NextGen AI Lab at BU. My research interests mainly focus on Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing. Through several impactful research projects in these areas, I have developed both analytical and creative approaches to problem-solving.

My long-term goal is to build computational systems with a broader and more coherent understanding, where vision models, language models, and generative frameworks work in concert rather than in isolation. I aim to develop techniques that not only interpret images and text but also ground their reasoning in real-world structure across diverse application domains, enabling more reliable and context-aware AI systems. I am committed to conducting rigorous, forward-looking research that contributes meaningfully to this direction.

I am currently looking for a Ph.D. position to contribute to cutting-edge research in Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing and to prepare for a career in advanced research in academia or industry.

For more information about my work and experience, please take a look at my resume and publications.
