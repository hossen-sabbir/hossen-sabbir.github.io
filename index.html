About

Sabbir Hossen is a Computer Science graduate passionate about Machine Learning, especially in Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing.

Sabbir Hossen is a recent Computer Science graduate from Bangladesh University with a strong foundation in Machine Learning and Deep Learning. His research interests are focused on Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing. He has demonstrated an analytical and creative approach to problem-solving through several impactful research projects in these domains. Known for being self-motivated, diligent, and an optimistic thinker, Sabbir continuously seeks opportunities to expand his expertise. He is looking for a Ph.D. position, aiming to contribute to cutting-edge research in Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing, and prepare for a career in academia or industry.
